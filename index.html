Red Fox Labs

Information on Red Fox Labs

Red Fox Labs are variants of the Labrador Retriever breed. They are a kind of gun dog. Gun dogs are dogs that are used to help hunters find and retrieve whatever they are hunting, usually birds. Labradors Retrievers are the most popular dog breed in Australia, Canada, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, and the United States. They are strong swimers because of the webbing inbetween the toes on their paws. Labradors love to be active and love playing in the water no matter the temperature because their coats protect them. They typically come in black, brown, and yellow. Red Fox Labs aren't very common.

Why Red Fox Labs are great pets

  • They're adorable
  • They're loyal
  • They're very excited all the time
  • Playful
  • Love to cuddle

Service and Military Labradors

Name What they did
Endall Endal is one of the most decorated dogs in the world. He won the "Dog of the Millennium" and the PDSA's Gold Medal for Animal Gallantry and Devotion to Duty. He was the first dog to work a 'chip and pin' ATM card.
Sarbi Sarbi is an Australian special forces explosives detection dog that spent almost 14 months missing in action in Afghanistan before being recovered safe and well in 2009
Jake Jake served as a search and rescue dog following the September 11 attacks and Hurricane Katrina.
Salty and Roselle Salty and Roselle were awarded the Dickin Medal for devotion to duty while serving in military conflict for leading their blind owners down more than 70 flights of stairs to escape from the damaged World Trade Center in September 2001
